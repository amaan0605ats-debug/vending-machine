Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common inquiries about our smart vending kiosks, payments, and delivery setup.

What payment methods do these machines accept? +
All our Smart Vending Kiosks are fully integrated with UPI/QR codes (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.). Higher-end models also support debit/credit card readers and traditional cash/coin receptors.
How does the remote inventory management system work? +
Every machine comes with an integrated IoT cellular modem that syncs live data with a central cloud dashboard. You can track sales, stock levels, temperature settings, and product expiry dates from your phone or laptop in real-time.
Do you provide delivery and physical setup services? +
Yes! We handle safe transport, on-site setup, and telemetry calibration. Our technical team also provides basic training for your staff to load and configure the machine.
What is the warranty period on VendX machines? +
All machines include a comprehensive 1-year hardware warranty. We also offer extended maintenance plans that cover regular cleaning, parts replacement, and software updates.
Can I customize the branding and wrapping? +
Absolutely. We can wrap the machine with your custom brand design, company logo, and custom digital advertisements to run on the high-definition touch display.
